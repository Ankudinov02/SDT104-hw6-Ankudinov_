Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What exactly is included in the booking price?
A: Accommodation in a private cozy cabin, unlimited hot coffee and artisan cocoa, warm chunky blankets, and free access to all snowy forest trails

Q: Is there internet connection on the property?
A: We highly encourage a "digital detox" so you can fully enjoy the silence of the mountains. However, the main lodge has stable Wi-Fi for emergency communication or work if absolutely necessary.

Q: What should I definitely bring with me?
A: Your favorite chunky sweater, comfortable winter walking boots, and a good book. We've taken care of everything else, including fresh towels and essential toiletries
